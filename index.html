BIOGRAPHY

‘Still I Rise’ – these are the words emblazoned across the back of Lewis Hamilton’s helmet and tattooed across his shoulders, and ever since annihilating expectations with one of the greatest rookie performances in F1 history in 2007, that’s literally all he’s done: risen to the top of the all-time pole positions list ahead of his hero Ayrton Senna, surged into first place in the wins column surpassing the inimitable Michael Schumacher, and then matched the legendary German’s seven world titles.

Is he the G.O.A.T? Few would deny that he’s in the conversation – and what’s more he’s got there his way, twinning his relentless speed with a refusal to conform to stereotypes for how a racing driver should think, dress or behave.

Respect is hard earned in F1, but Hamilton – now Lewis Hamilton to be precise – has it from every one of his peers. Why? Because they know that whatever the track, whatever the conditions, whatever the situation, when his visor goes down and the lights go out, it’s Hammertime.

Formula One Career

Mclaren (2007-2012)

Lewis Hamilton's career with McLaren in Formula 1, starting in 2007, was marked by immediate success and a rapid rise to stardom. As a rookie, he showcased remarkable talent, securing podium finishes and claiming his first win in his debut season. The pinnacle of his McLaren years came in 2008 when Hamilton clinched the World Championship in a thrilling season finale. His time with McLaren, lasting until the end of 2012, saw consistent competitiveness, numerous race victories, and established him as one of the sport's brightest talents before he moved on to Mercedes for the 2013 season.

Mercedes (2013-2024)

Lewis Hamilton's tenure with Mercedes-AMG in Formula 1, which began in 2013, has been nothing short of historic. Joining forces with the Silver Arrows, Hamilton has achieved unparalleled success, dominating the sport in a manner reminiscent of his idol, Ayrton Senna. Since the switch, Hamilton has claimed multiple World Championships, solidifying his status as one of the greatest drivers in Formula 1 history. His partnership with Mercedes has yielded numerous race wins, breaking records for pole positions and podium finishes. Beyond his on-track prowess, Hamilton has been a prominent advocate for social and environmental issues, using his platform to address important causes. The collaboration between Lewis Hamilton and Mercedes-AMG stands as one of the most successful and influential partnerships in modern Formula 1.

Ferrari (upcoming 2025 season)

Lewis Hamilton's move to Scuderia Ferrari in 2025 marks a significant chapter in the history of Formula 1. The announcement of Hamilton's switch from Mercedes to Ferrari has sent shockwaves through the motorsport world, setting the stage for a new era of competition. The partnership between Hamilton and Ferrari is poised to redefine the landscape of Formula 1, as the seven-time World Champion embarks on a new journey with the iconic Italian team. The anticipation surrounding Hamilton's arrival at Ferrari is palpable, as fans and enthusiasts eagerly await the unveiling of the new era of Formula 1.
